How it started ?
Our journey began with a vision, sparked by the experiences of our founding team, a group of former Airbnb professionals. As avid travellers themselves, they noticed a significant shift in travel preferences, with more travellers choosing vacation homes over hotels. However, they observed that vacation homes lacked consistent and professional hospitality and fell short in providing the level of service and care that guests truly deserved.
Future Goals
We believe vacations should be moments of pure indulgence, where guests are pampered and made to feel special. This belief laid the very foundation for Staymaster - to elevate the standards of vacation rental hospitality and customer service, where every aspect of a guest's stay is meticulously taken care of, ensuring their experience is predictable, seamless, and memorable.
