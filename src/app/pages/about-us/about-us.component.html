Property Curation Process
Just like our folks, our homes too go through an interview process. No kidding! Personality, quality and comfort are the values we look for in every home before we have it on our portfolio.

Each property undergoes a rigorous 200+ point inspection to ensure safety, comfort, and unparalleled quality.
The First Impressions
We assess the potential of any home considering its location, neighbourhoodand amenities. We don't stop at homes,we rather start looking for unique experiences it could offer to guests.
Thorough Evaluation
The spotlight's brighter to see if comfort, aesthetics and functionality seamlessly converge at a home. Elegant spaces, eye-catching artwork or thoughtful touches that transform a home to haven, we love it!
Safety & Security

How it Started?
Having a spare room transitioned into a business idea for Ankur Mathur and Rishi Modi. Both seasoned travellers with a passion for hosting had opened the doors of their homes to several visitors. Sharing stories and learning about cultures turned into perfecting the art of hosting and offering guest experiences. Having worked with Airbnb, they saw firsthand the joys and challenges of creating exceptional stays and thus the idea of mastering the smarter way to stay was born in 2021.
Future Goals
As the lines between travel and living blur among new-age travellers, Staymaster aims to be the bridge. We present homes where travellers don't just stay—they live and truly belong. Setting a strong base from Goa to steadily moving across the major travel destinations in the country, we envision a world where life inspired by wanderlust finds solace in just belonging to a certain place.

In our future, every traveller finds a haven away from home, and every homeowner gains a partner they can trust their abode with
